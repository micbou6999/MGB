About the Check Engine Light

The "Check Engine" light is an important indicator that there may be a problem with your vehicle's engine or emissions system. It can be triggered by a range of issues, both minor and serious.

Common reasons for the "Check Engine" light include issues with the oxygen sensor, catalytic converter, spark plugs, or the gas cap.

Our Services

At MGB Car Company, we offer comprehensive services to diagnose and address "Check Engine" light issues:

1. Diagnostic Code Reading: We use advanced diagnostic tools to retrieve trouble codes and pinpoint the specific issue.

2. Emission System Inspection: Our experts will examine components like the oxygen sensor and catalytic converter to ensure proper function.

3. Spark Plug and Ignition System Check: We'll assess spark plugs, coils, and related components for signs of wear or malfunction.

4. Fuel System Evaluation: We'll inspect the fuel delivery system to ensure proper fuel pressure and injection.

5. Gas Cap Inspection: A loose or faulty gas cap can trigger the "Check Engine" light; we'll check and replace if needed.
