Our Routine Maintenance Services

Regular routine maintenance is essential to keep your vehicle running smoothly. At MGB Car Company, we specialize in the following services:

  • Brake Inspections and Replacements: Properly functioning brakes are crucial for your safety on the road. We provide comprehensive brake inspections and replacements to ensure your vehicle stops effectively.
  • Air Filter Replacements: A clean air filter allows your engine to breathe properly, improving fuel efficiency and performance. We recommend regular air filter replacements to maintain optimal engine health.
  • Cabin Air Filter Replacements: Clean cabin air filters help maintain a healthy interior environment by filtering out dust, pollen, and other particles. We ensure your cabin air filter is in top condition for your comfort.
  • Ignition Coil Checks and Replacements: Faulty ignition coils can lead to misfires and reduced engine performance. Our expert technicians will inspect and replace ignition coils as needed to keep your engine running smoothly.
  • Spark Plug Replacements: Spark plugs play a crucial role in igniting the air-fuel mixture in your engine. We recommend regular spark plug replacements to maintain optimal fuel efficiency and performance.

By staying on top of these routine maintenance services, you can ensure that your vehicle operates at its best, providing you with a safe and reliable driving experience.

Please note that these are the only routine maintenance services we offer at MGB Car Company.
