Common Reasons for No Crank

When your car doesn't crank, it can be caused by various factors. Here are some common issues:

1. Dead Battery: A discharged or faulty battery can prevent the car from starting.

2. Starter Motor Failure: The starter motor may be malfunctioning and unable to turn the engine.

3. Ignition Switch Problems: A faulty ignition switch can prevent electrical power from reaching the starter.

4. Faulty Starter Solenoid: The solenoid may not be sending electrical current to the starter motor.

5. Issues with Wiring or Connections: Corroded or damaged wiring can lead to a loss of electrical continuity.
