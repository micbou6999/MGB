Mission Statement

At MGB Car Company, our mission is to deliver accessible and budget-friendly maintenance and oil change services. Our commitment goes beyond profit margins; we prioritize affordability to ensure that quality automotive care is within everyone's reach. We believe that every vehicle owner deserves reliable and cost-effective solutions. With a focus on providing value, our goal is to keep you and your vehicle on the road without breaking the bank.

Important Note on Repairs

At MGB Car Company, we understand that when servicing vehicles, unforeseen issues may arise. Not every vehicle has received proper maintenance over time, which can lead to unexpected complications.

During the course of our service, it is possible to discover issues that were not initially apparent. These could range from damaged brake calipers which hold the brake pads, to issues such as a broken oil filter housing or a stripped oil drain plug.

Rest assured that if we encounter any unexpected repairs, we will promptly inform you and discuss the best course of action to ensure your vehicle's safety and performance. Our goal is to provide you with the most comprehensive and reliable service possible.
